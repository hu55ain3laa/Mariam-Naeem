OcnoBrain AI

Description

The project focuses on using Artificial Intelligence (AI) to diagnose brain tumors through MRI images and raise awareness about different types of brain tumors. It aims to provide a diagnostic process, including early symptom identification, tumor types, and preventive measures. The project is designed to assist doctors, medical students and raise awareness among the general public.
